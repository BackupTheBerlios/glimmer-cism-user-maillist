mostly just a hello, but partly scouting for advice. I'm working with 
Jonathan Gregory at the University of Reading on a project that involves 
improving icesheet-atmosphere model coupling.

Jonathan's had the UK Met Office Unified Model (UM) coupled to an old 
version of Glimmer, running on the UK HPCx and HECToR supercomputers, 
for a while now, but we're looking to install a newer version of 
Glimmer-CISM on our local servers to use as a base for this project.

for a particular version of G-C to download or avoid? I've been advised
