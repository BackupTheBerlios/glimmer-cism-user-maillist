Hi Oliver,

I work on the northern hemisphere ice sheet in late quaternary. I have 
simulated the Laurentide and Fennoscandian ice sheet at the LGM with 
Glimmer. I use a fast GCM called FAMOUS to force Glimmer. I would be 
happy to compare my results with yours.

Regarding the initial condition, studies in the litterature usually 
ignore the fact that the present day topography is not relaxed. The 
approximation is small compared to all the other assumptions you make, 
especially for the LGM. If you use a reconstruction like Peltier's you 
will end up with a very smooth bedrock. And the bedrock really shapes 
the ice streams so I recommend using the present day bedrock as the  the 
relaxed bedrock if you want your ice streams to look good.

I can confirm the problem with the marine_margin=4 option, I had some 
funny results with it. I use the option marine_margin=2.

Don't hesitate to email me if you want to know more about my work.

Lauren
