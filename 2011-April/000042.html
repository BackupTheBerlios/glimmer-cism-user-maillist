Hi All
 
I  tried the initialization for Greenland and Antarctic. I did  everything
following the web page(http://websrv.cs.umt.edu/Isis/index
php/Configuration_Files). However the results were unreasonable, and
differed with the results in the web page . 
 
The forcing dara are the 120000-year (-120000---0BP) temeprature, surface
mass balance and sea level change.
 
In Antarctic the ice volume kept increasing in the 120000-year run. There
was no oscillation like the glacial-interglacial circle. And in 120000
(model time equivalent to preindustrial?), the ice area was too large.
 
In Greenland the precipitation was always zero, same as ice volume and ice
area, except the first time step.
 
 
The model used is glimmer-1.7.0. I compiled it as follows: /configure
--prefix=/home/nersc/yanq/glimmer-1.7.0  --with-netcdf=/opt/Cray/netcdf/4.1
1.0/netcdf-pgi FC=pgf90 F77=pgf90
 
I am wondering if anyone could give me some suggestions. Thank you very
much! 
 
Regards
 
Qing
