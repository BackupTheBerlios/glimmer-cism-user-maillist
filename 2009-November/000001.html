what compiler are you using? 

You could enable debugging info by adding FCFLAGS="-C -g" to the end of
your configure command. You will need to do a make clean && make
afterwards to recompile everything. That way you can figure out where it
breaks. Also the code you are using is slightly out-of-date. The new
place is
http://svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/glimmer-cism/glimmer-cism-lanl/trunk/
your problem might be fixed there.
