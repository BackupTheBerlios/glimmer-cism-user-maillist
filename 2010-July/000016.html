> You shouldn't see thickness changes that big on the first timestep. It
> might be a scaling problem. The ice thickness in glimmer is scaled by a
> factor of thk0 = 2000, so if you have acab = 0.5 m/yr and something is
> scaled incorrectly, you could see a 1000-m change in thickness. (I look
> forward to when those scaling factors are gone.)
